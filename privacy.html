5. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard methods. Even Petaraa staff cannot read them.
  • Database security: Firestore Security Rules ensure users can only access their own data. Every request is verified.
  • Secure connections: All data transfers use HTTPS encryption.
  • API key security: Secret keys are stored on the server, never exposed in the app.
  • Payment security: All payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant). We never store card numbers, CVV, or UPI PINs.
  • Access control: Only authorized personnel can access backend systems.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data — View all your information in your profile and settings within the app.
  • Edit your data — Update your profile, skills, bio, photo, and preferences anytime.
  • Delete your account — Go to Settings → Delete Account. Your profile and personal data will be removed within 30 days.
  • Download your data — Email us at petaraahq@gmail.com and we will provide a copy of your data.
  • Opt out of notifications — Toggle notification preferences in Settings → Notifications.
  • Opt out of personalized ads — Adjust ad preferences in your device settings (controlled by Google, not Petaraa).

7. Cookies & Tracking

Petaraa is a mobile app, not a website — we do not use browser cookies. We may use standard mobile analytics tools to understand app usage and improve the experience. These tools collect anonymized, aggregated data — not personally identifiable information.

8. Children's Privacy

Petaraa is designed for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that an underage user has created an account, we will promptly delete the account and associated data.

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Your data is kept for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Personal data is removed within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Community content: Posts and comments may remain in anonymized form after account deletion.
  • Financial records: Payment and transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by applicable tax and financial laws.
  • Suspended accounts: Data from suspended accounts may be retained to prevent the user from creating new accounts.

10. International Users

Petaraa is available globally. Your data is processed and stored on servers managed by Firebase (Google Cloud). By using Petaraa, you consent to your data being processed in accordance with this policy, regardless of your country of residence.
