Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026 · Last Updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it. Written in simple language — no legal jargon.

1. What Data We Collect

When You Sign Up

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted — we cannot see it)
  • Account type (Jobseeker or Employer)

When You Use the App (Jobseeker)

  • Profile info: city, bio, skills, photo
  • Job applications: cover notes, screening answers
  • Content: posts, comments, stories, videos
  • Learning progress: courses completed, XP earned
  • Wallet: transaction history, withdrawal requests
  • Chat messages (between you and other users)
  • AI conversations (with Nexi assistant)

When You Use the App (Employer)

  • Company info: name, contact person, industry, city, size, logo
  • Job posts: title, description, salary, requirements
  • Applicant interactions: shortlist/reject decisions, private notes

Automatically Collected

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage patterns
  • IP address (for security)

5. How We Protect Your Data

  • Passwords are encrypted — even we cannot read them.
  • Database access is protected by security rules.
  • API keys are stored securely on the server.
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — See your data in your profile and settings.
  • Edit — Update your profile, skills, bio anytime.
  • Delete — Settings → Delete Account removes your data.
  • Download — Contact petaraahq@gmail.com for your data.
  • Opt out — Toggle notifications in Settings.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Petaraa is a mobile app — we don't use browser cookies. We may use standard mobile analytics to improve the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Petaraa is for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. Underage accounts will be deleted.

9. Data Retention

  • Data is kept as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: personal data removed within 30 days.
  • Some content may remain in anonymized form.
  • Payment records kept for 7 years (legal requirement).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Petaraa grows. Significant changes will be notified in the app. Continued use means you accept updates.

11. Contact Us

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at: petaraahq@gmail.com
