2. Gig Orders (Marketplace)

2.1 How the Escrow System Works

All gig orders on Petaraa use an escrow system. When you order a gig, your payment is held securely until the delivery process is complete. This protects both buyers and sellers.

  1. Buyer places order → payment held in escrow
  2. Seller completes the work and submits delivery
  3. Buyer reviews the work
  4. Buyer approves → payment released to seller (minus Petaraa's 15% commission)

2.2 When You GET a Refund

  • Seller did not deliver — If the seller fails to deliver the work within the agreed deadline AND after using their one-time extension, the buyer receives a full refund.
  • Dispute resolved in buyer's favor — If a dispute is raised and Petaraa's review determines the seller did not fulfill the order requirements, the buyer receives a full or partial refund based on the situation.
  • Technical error — If payment was charged but the order was not created due to a technical issue, a full refund is issued.

2.3 When You DO NOT Get a Refund

  • Buyer approved the delivery — Once you confirm that the work is satisfactory and approve the delivery, payment is released. No refund after approval.
  • Buyer did not respond within 5 days — If the seller submits delivery and the buyer does not respond (approve, reject, or request revision) within 5 days, payment is automatically released to the seller. No refund after auto-release.
  • "Changed my mind" — If you ordered a gig and simply changed your mind after the seller has started working, this is not eligible for a refund.

2.4 Delivery Extensions

If a seller needs more time to deliver:

  • The seller communicates with the buyer via in-app chat
  • If the buyer agrees, the seller requests a one-time deadline extension through Petaraa
  • Only one extension per order is allowed
  • The extension requires buyer approval
  • If delivery is still not completed after the extension, the buyer is eligible for a full refund

2.5 Revisions

Before approving or raising a dispute, the buyer may request up to 2 revisions from the seller. The seller is expected to make reasonable changes based on the original order requirements.

2.6 Disputes

If the buyer and seller cannot agree:

  • Either party can raise a dispute through the app
  • Petaraa will review the case online — based on chat history, order details, and submitted work
  • Petaraa's decision is fair and final
  • Resolution may be: full refund, partial refund, or release of payment to seller
  • No physical meeting required — everything is handled digitally

3. Creator Courses

No refund on course purchases.

Once a course is purchased, it becomes permanently accessible to the buyer. The buyer can view the course content at any time, at their own pace. Since access is granted immediately and permanently, no refunds are available.

This protects creators from unfair losses — a buyer cannot consume the content and then request a refund.

4. Employer Job Credits

No refund on job credits.

Once purchased, job credits are available in your account and can be used at any time to post job listings. Credits do not expire. Since credits are a stored value that you can use whenever you choose, no refunds are available.

Exception: If a technical issue caused a payment to be charged without credits being added to your account, a full refund will be issued.

5. Employer Job Boost

No refund after boost activation.

Once a job boost (Regular 7-day or Featured 14-day) is purchased and activated, the service begins immediately. Since the boost is a time-based service that starts right away, no refunds are available after activation.

6. Technical Issues & Payment Errors

Full refund for genuine technical issues.

If any of the following occur, you are eligible for a full refund regardless of the purchase type:

  • Payment was charged but the service/product was not delivered or activated due to a technical error
  • You were charged multiple times for the same purchase
  • A system error caused an incorrect charge amount

Contact us immediately with your payment details and we will resolve it.

7. How to Request a Refund

  1. Send an email to petaraahq@gmail.com with:
    • Your registered email address
    • Order ID or transaction ID
    • Reason for the refund request
    • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, chat history)
  2. Our team will review your request within 48 hours.
  3. If approved, the refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method (the same UPI, card, or bank account you paid from).

8. Fraudulent Refund Requests

Petaraa takes fraud seriously. If a refund request is found to be:

  • Dishonest or based on false claims
  • An attempt to receive services for free
  • Part of a pattern of repeated refund abuse

The request will be denied, and the user's account may be subject to warning or permanent suspension as described in our Terms of Use.

9. Transaction Fees on Refunds

When a refund is issued, payment processing fees (charged by Razorpay) may be deducted from the refund amount. This is because payment processors typically do not return their processing fee. In most cases, you will receive the full amount back, but in some cases a small processing fee may apply.
